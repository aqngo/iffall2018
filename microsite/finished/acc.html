Awards and Accolades

Amy Poehler has won a number of awards throughout her career. Here is a list of all the major awards and accolades she has won since starting at Saturday Night Live. (this list does not include awards that Amy was nominated for but did not win)

2005

    Teen Choice Awards– Choice Comedian
    (Saturday Night Live)

2009

    MTV Movie Awards– Best WTF Moment
    (Baby Mama)

2011

    Variety– Power of Comedy Award
    (Career Achievement)

2012

    The Comedy Awards– Best Comedy Actress
    (Parks and Recreation)
    Critics' Choice Television Awards– Best Actress in a Comedy Series
    (Parks and Recreation)

2013

    Gracie Awards– Outstanding Female Actor in a Leading Role, Comedy
    (Parks and Recreation)

2014

    Golden Globe Awards– Best Actress, Television Series Musical or Comedy
    (Parks and Recreation)

2015

    Writers Guild of America– Comedy/Variety (Music, Awards, Tributes), Specials
    (Parks and Recreation)

2016

    Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Awards– Favorite Voice in an Animated Movie
    (Inside Out)
    Primetime Emmy Awards– Outstanding Guest Actress in a Comedy Series
    (Saturday Night Live)
