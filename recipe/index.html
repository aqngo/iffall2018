Preparing Maruchan Ramen

Watch This First

Ingredients

  1. Maruchan Ramen, 1 Package (includes dry noodles, seasoning packet)
  2. Water, 2 Cups
  3. Saucepan

Cooking

  1. Boil 2 Cups water in saucepan
  2. Add noodles and cook 3 minutes, or until noodles are soft.
    Stir occasionally.
  3. Turn off heat. Add contents of seasoning package. stir well.
  4. Serve hot.

Tips

– To make the dish less salty, use less of the seasoning packet.
– Ramen can be eaten as noodle soup, or the water can be drained.
– Ramen can be eaten as a quick snack, or it can be the base for delcious meals.
– Vegetables like spinach or bok choy can be cooked with the soup– simply add them at the same time as the noodles.
– Cooked noodles can be used as the base for stir fry or other dishes.
